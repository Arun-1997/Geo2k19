ஏன் ஏன் கத்துற

Know More


An event which comes with the promise of fun and enjoyment. It is primarily a pictionary and dumb charades mixed altogether to enthrall the participants.


ROUND 1:

DATE AND TIME: March 9, 2019 - (11.30 AM to 12.30 PM)
VENUE: Room No 11, Institute of Remote Sensing, Anna University

Say what you see...a team may have 2 members. They should select a sheet from a bowl containing many similar ones. Each sheet will contain 2 or 3 dialogues and few words. One member of the team will be wearing earphones, and loud music will be played. The other one should tell the dialogues and words in the sheet to the one wearing earphones within the given time. If the dialogues and words are said back correctly the team goes to the next round

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:

  • A team may have 2 members
  • Lowering the volume or moving the earphones will mean disqualification.
  • The dialogue shall not be said more than thrice and the whole dialogue must be said. Breaking it into parts and repeating is not allowed.
  • Using hand signs or gestures is not allowed.

ROUND 2: SACK IT UP

DATE AND TIME: March 9, 2019 - (1.30 AM to 2.30 PM)
VENUE: Room No 11, Institute of Remote Sensing, Anna University

Rush to collect 'em...6 sacks will be placed around the IRS building each containing different materials. A list of clues will be given to a team. Each clue describes a material. They should find what the material is and pick it from the sacks before the time ends. The team that collects all or most of the materials given to them wins.

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED:
  • Only one member of a team should search in a sack.
  • Throwing the materials out of the sack or placing them down for searching is not allowed.
  • Other team members will also be searching in the same sack.so there should not be any quarrel between the teams.
  • Each team will get unique clues.so they shouldn't claim other team's materials.
