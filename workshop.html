UAV Workshop

Details


Interested in trying your hands at UAV and aerial image processing but don't know where to start from ?

The Society of Geoinformatics engineers offers a workshop "UAV mapping and processing", a certified training program, as part of GeoHorizon' 19, a national level technical symposium, to provide a steep learning curve in advancing aerial image mapping with UAV. This workshop would enable the participants to develop a perspicacious understanding of operating UAV and processing the images acquired of the study area during its operation.

The participants would be ferried to Semmancheri area. The first session entails hands on experience in operating the UAV and collecting aerial images of the area, post a demonstrative session. The second session entails training on processing the images and data acquired during the previous session, using a software in one of the laboratories of the Institute of Remote Sensing.

Certification

A certificate of completion would be provided for all the participants who successfully complete the workshop.
